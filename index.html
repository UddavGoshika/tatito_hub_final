International Voice Process

Our International Voice Process is a structured healthcare verification and customer confirmation workflow, primarily supporting US-based diabetic healthcare and Medicare-related service providers. Agents securely log in using client-issued credentials and access real-time customer dashboards. The process involves verifying essential customer details displayed on the screen, including first name, last name, address, ZIP code, and eligibility information. Agents interact with customers through inbound and outbound calls using compliance-approved scripts to confirm the accuracy of the information. Once verification and customer consent are completed, the validated data is securely submitted into the client‚Äôs lead management system. Each verified submission is treated as a qualified lead. The entire process follows strict quality control, login‚Äìwork‚Äìlogout protocols, and transparent call disposition tracking to maintain compliance and client trust.

Non-Voice Data Entry

The International Non-Voice Data Entry process is a high-accuracy, offline CRM-based project designed for large-volume data processing. Operators are required to complete 5,000 forms per seat within a 20-working-day cycle, divided into two structured submissions of 2,500 forms each. Each form contains approximately 36 predefined fields that must be entered according to strict client guidelines. Accuracy plays a critical role in payout eligibility, and all submissions undergo quality validation before acceptance. Tatito Projects provides demo forms prior to live deployment, along with complete training and ongoing technical support. The project operates Monday to Saturday and follows a transparent execution model, making it suitable for organizations seeking reliable, process-driven non-voice operations.
